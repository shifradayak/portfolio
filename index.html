Hi, I'm Shifra!

I'm a reporter who's studying journalism and environmental science at the University of Maryland. I love telling people-centered stories through writing, graphics and video, and my interests lie in reporting on environmental issues and politics.

Currently, I'm news editor at my school's independent student publication, The Diamondback, where I spearhead and delegate news coverage of the greater College Park community to a desk of 13 writers. This summer, I'm a reporting intern at The Frederick News-Post, where I've contributed to breaking news and feature coverage of Frederick County, including reporting on public records, county and city government and environment.

I was born and raised in Montgomery County, Md. Outside of journalism, I enjoy reading, hiking and cooking. Feel free to reach out and chat!
